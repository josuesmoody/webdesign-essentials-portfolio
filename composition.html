Composition Techniques

Introduction

Composition techniques are vital for photographers because they help structure an image in a way that is visually appealing, guides the viewer's attention, and conveys the intended message or emotion effectively.

Top 3 Composition Techniques

  • 1. Rule of Thirds - Divide your frame into 9 equal parts and place your subject at the intersections. View Example
  • 2. Leading Lines - Use lines to guide the viewer's eye through the scene. View Example
  • 3. Symmetry - Create balance and harmony by mirroring elements. View Example
